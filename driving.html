Slow Down

Most cars achieve their best fuel efficiency at around 50mph. Also, don't hammer on the accelerator and jam on the brakes - nice and easy does it!

Pump it Up

Keep your tyres inflated to the correct pressure. Less air in the tyres means more friction, and more friction means the engine burns more juice!

Don't Travel Alone

If you add one more person to your car, you'll cut your fuel cost in half if they contribute to the cost of the trip! Be sure to get a doughnut in the bargain to cover maintainance!
